My Favorite Restaurants in St. Louis

My hometown of St. Louis has a lot of really good options for restaurants, with an amazing variety of food from all over the world. While some of these restaurants are chains, I will do my best to avoid them.

Seoul Taco

6665 Delmar Blvd, St. Louis, MO 63130
(314) 863-1148
seoultaco.com

Seoul Taco is one of my favorite restaurants in the Loop and the perfect place to sit down and eat after a long day of shopping.

Gingham's Homestyle Restaurant

1566 Country Club Plaza Dr, St. Charles, MO 63303
(636) 946-0266
ginghamsrestaurant.com

Gingham's is the last diner of its kind in the St. Louis area, as it is open 24/7, and has some of the best food of its kind in the area. With it being so close to Main Street and the Ameristar, it is a perfect place to wind down after a night out.

Salt + Smoke

501 S Main St, St Charles, MO 63301
(314) 727-0200
saltandsmokebbq.com

501 S Main St, St Charles, MO 63301
(314) 727-0200
saltandsmokebbq.com

Another great spot near Main St, serving some of the most amazing barbeque I have ever had, a great place to go for a date night (not the first as it can get a little messy).

Sweetie Cup

2961 Dougherty Ferry Rd, Kirkwood, MO 63122
(636) 529-8690

The best most affordable Thai food in all of St. Louis, their Pad Thai and Pad Kee Mao are to die for.
